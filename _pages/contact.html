--- layout: page title: Contact permalink: /contact/ image: '/images/37.jpg' --- Dr. Keyu Jin is an associate professor (with tenure) of Economics at the London school of Economics. She is the author of The New China Playbook. Keyu is from Beijing, China, and holds a B.A., M.A. and Phd from Harvard University. Her research is on international economics--- such as why capital flows from poor to rich countries, how the global implications of U.S. monetary and fiscal policies have changed; as well as on how the rise of China impacts the global economy, from several perspectives ---trade, capital flows, global interest rates and saving, demographics, productivity and technology.
